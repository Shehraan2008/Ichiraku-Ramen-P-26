# Ingrediant for the Ramen.

---

## Main Ingrediants

- Two packs of ramen noodles of your choice.
- 5 cups of pork or beef broth (You can use the stuff in the package as a substitute.)
- Pork Tenderloin
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Baby bok choy
- 1 Green Onion
- Soy Sauce
- Aburage x5
- Nori
- Kamaboko x3
